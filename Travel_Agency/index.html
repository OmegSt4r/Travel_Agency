What To Wear


Your trip includes professional guides, raingear, rubber boots and a
small snack. Dress warmly in layers as conditions around the mountains can be
chilly. Please bring thick socks(to wear with provided rubber boots), warm
clothes(wear synthetic layers and bring a rain coat), warm hat and gloves.
You may wish to bring binoculars and a camera if you have them.
